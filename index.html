Welcome to Simply Anais

More than just a spa

What we have to offer

At Simply Anais, wellness is our specialty. The spa has been around since 2005 and is the heart of the Midlands. We have customers come and go expressing their joy of being part of this wonderful experience.
Our staff are trained to the highest level providing you with all the luxury treatments at a very high standard. We provide single spa days for a quick treatment option, spa breaks for upto 3 days with morning breakfast and evening tea, and finally a wellbeing retreat for upto 4 people where you will stay with us for 5 days enjoying what we have to offer at the spa along with morning breakfast, afternoon dinner and evening tea with the option to join in with evening entertainment.
Come, take in the surroundings and explore your luxury side.

Treatments

With our highly trained staff you will receive fantastic customer service along with high attention to detail treatments from soothing massages ranging from full body to specific limbs, rejuvenating facials with highly rated cosmetic creams and lotions, or sprucing up your nails with manicures and pedicures with an array of gels and colours that suit your needs.

Spa Facilities

Enjoy what we have on offer with our top of the range spa facilities from our two big size swimming pools along with our spa whirlpool to kick your muscles back to life. Sweat out all the toxins in our suitable size saunas that can hold up to four people. Lastly not forgetting our newly implemented ice fountain used for hot and cold therapy to finish off with style.

Classes

Not only do we provide relaxing treatments, we offer classes from gym sessions with your own PT providing you with a plan for the days you are with us. Spin classes for an intense biking session. Aerobics and aqua aerobics in our sports halls and swimming pool. To finish off we have plenty of yoga events for helping flexibility and maintaining a balance metabolism.

Retreats

We have drop in treatments where you could have that odd relaxing massage after a long day at work or to jazz up your nails for a night out. Spa breaks for couples or friends upto 3 days where you can choose treatments for each day along with breakfast and evening tea. Finally if you want the full package then our Wellbeing retreat will provide you with unlimited treatments including all facilities and classes along with breakfast, dinner and tea.

What packages we have on offer

One Day

For our one day package option, you have the choice to come in for 1 or more treatments. Treatments have to total an hour minimum if you choose this package and have a maximum of 3 adults to make sure you get the full experience.

Benefits

  • Great for a one off treatment
  • No wait time as we will let you know right away of any free slots
  • Fit in any time of the day up until 6pm
  • Share the joy with upto 3 friends
  • Enjoy a professional treatment with minimal cost

Cost: Dependant on treatment

Spa Retreat

Do you want a retreat where you can enjoy a good few days of pampering? Then a Spa Retreat Package is just for you. With up to 3 adults you can enjoy a luxury retreat for up to 3 days with us where you get the 2 treatments a day with morning breakfast and evening tea.

Benefits

  • Great for a weekend full of treatments
  • Use of the facilities such as pool and sauna
  • Morning breakfast with evening tea
  • Multiple treatments of your choice
  • Choice of one class per group

Cost: £195pp

Wellbeing Retreat

If you would like the full package then choose the Wellbeing Retreat where you get unlimited treatments including all classes and facilities. This is for upto 3 people and 5 nights where again you get morning breakfast, evening tea and the choice of dinner as well.

Benefits

  • Unlimited treatments
  • Access to all facilities and classes
  • 5 days of professional treatments with our well trained staff
  • Morning breakfast, evening tea and a choice of dinner in our top restaurant
  • 1 to 1 wellbeing life pack on your personal care

Cost: £450pp
